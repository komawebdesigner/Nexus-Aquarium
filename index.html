Welcome To Nexus Aquarium

We are a non-profit group dedicated to Conservation of Aquatic life, Ocean Exploration and Marine Life Conservation.
Situated in Washington DC, our Aquarium is home to over 5000 aquatic animals including the beautiful yet
extremely dangerous blue-ringed octopus.Enjoy a remarkable journey and learn about the different varieties of aquatic animals
on display.Plan a visit with us and experience the sea like you have never before.

 

Come visit our aquarium and learn from our expert Marine Biologists and Scientists, attend our short lectures
and get to learn about Marine Life. Experience our underwater tunnel aquarium and get to see the
variety of the sea creatures like never seen before. Know about our events for both adults and
children, bring your kids for our weekend events since we have set the weekend specifically for them.
